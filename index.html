About

Currently, I am a postdoctoral researcher at Department of Computer Science and Engineering, The Chinese University of Hong Kong (CUHK). I received my Ph.D. degree from CUHK in 2025, supervised by Prof. Bei Yu. Prior to that, I received my B.Eng. in Information Engineering at Zhejiang University (ZJU) in 2021. My research interests include VLSI CAD, 3D Integrated Circuits, Machine Learning in EDA, and GPU Acceleration.

I will join the School of Science and Engineering at The Chinese University of Hong Kong, Shenzhen, as an Assistant Professor in June 2026, and I am seeking motivated M.Phil./Ph.D. students and Research Assistants with backgrounds in electronic engineering, computer science and engineering, applied mathematics, or related fields. If you are interested, please feel free to contact me by email.
